HOW I WORK
01
DISCOVER
Research the brand, audience, and context. Ask uncomfortable questions. Find the real brief underneath the brief.
02
DEFINE
Translate insights into a creative direction. Choose mood, palette, and visual language before touching Illustrator.
03
DESIGN
Explore obsessively. Save versions. Go back to old versions. Refine until the work feels inevitable.
04
DELIVER
Present with context, not just JPEGs. The story behind the work is as important as the work itself.
